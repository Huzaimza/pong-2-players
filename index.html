How to Play

  • Choose 1 Player (vs Computer) or 2 Player (vs a friend) mode.
  • Player 1 (Left Paddle): Use W (up) and S (down) or Up/Down Arrow keys, or on-screen arrows on mobile.
  • Player 2 (Right Paddle): Use Up/Down Arrow keys (only in 2 Player mode), or on-screen arrows on mobile.
  • Press Pause/Quit button or Esc key to pause and return to mode selection.
  • Score by getting the ball past your opponent's paddle.
  • First to reach your own chosen score wins (no limit by default).
  • If you choose a time limit, the game will end when time runs out. Highest score wins!
